Usage: quick-start [-n | -s | -d] [project_name] [directory]

  -n, --new      new project
  example: quick-start --new project_name ./my_new_project

  -s, --scan     scan project
  example: quick-start --scan project_name ./my_new_project

  -d, --delete   delete project
  example: quick-start --delete project_name

  -l, --list     show the list of your projects
